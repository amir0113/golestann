Golestan's

Saffron

Known as “red gold”, Saffron is planted primarily in some parts of the Iranian province of Khorasan. Golestan saffron is pure with a pleasant smell. Its color is scarlet with a bitter flavor. The finest saffron is the one that comes from Sargol in Khorasan Province. It is available in packs of different sizes.

Rice

Iranian rice is the most prime in the world. Tarom and Hashemi are the two most popular rice brands in the country. Tarom and Hashemi rice is grown in the best rice farmlands in the Iranian province of Mazandaran and after its quality is tested in Golestan laboratories, it is packed and sold.
